Who We Are

Strategic Links is a consulting firm focused on improving the performance capabilities and business results of our client organizations.

Strategic Links specializes in training individuals to bridge competence gaps. Founded in the year 2006 with the intent of integrating the latest learning methods available worldwide in learning methodologies, we partner with organizations and educational institutions to help them enhance performance and be more productive. Our expertise in preparing fresh talent for the corporate world and in enabling existing managers to be better leaders for their people. We provide performance and talent management solutions through efficient customized implementable consulting services. We are committed to partnering with our clients to ensure the right targeted approach to their organizational objectives, issues, culture environment. Our USP is our strong execution-focus and the level of customization that we offer.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to enable & enhance the eminence of individuals. We believe that everyone has the ability and the willingness to develop. The need is to identify their strengths and development areas to design the intervention that will lead to their desired levels of success to be able to integrate in all their endeavors & relationships.

OUR VALUES

Responsibility

We believe in the capability of humans to be able to carve a better future for themselves. We make ourselves available and take personal responsibility for this cause.

Authenticity

Trust is built on delivering promised results; hence we refrain from over-committing to issues or tasks that we think will not be delivered par excellence. We practice what we preach so as to constantly strive to remain true to our vision.

Meaningful Relationships

We value long-term relationships for mutual benefit that are founded on open communication, feedback and respect.

OUR APPROACH

Customized Content

Content to match the needs of the organization instead of predefined content. The customized content is not shared to secure the confidentiality and uniqueness of the organization

Simplicity & Discipline

Based on years of research and experience, the best programs are those that are easy to understand. Post identification and before intervention vigorous exercise is done to convert the complex into simple in order to make it application friendly

Follow up Sessions

Training isn't worthwhile if it isn't causing an impact on results. Compulsory follow up session to design learning journeys for individuals that handholds them till the training results are achieved.
